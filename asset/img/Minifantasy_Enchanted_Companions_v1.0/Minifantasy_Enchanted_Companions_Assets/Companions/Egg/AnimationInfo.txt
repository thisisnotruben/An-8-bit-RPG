*Frame size* 

- 32x32px: For all the animations.


*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: Hatching, Dmg, Die

- 200ms: Idle, Walk


Notes:

- This companion is a weird egg-like creature that, with time, can hatch transformed in nearly any other creature. 
- Protect it long enough until it hatches a friendly creature that fights for you.

- The Creature should appear at the 16th frame of Hatching animation.
- Place the chosen creature Idle animation under the Hatching layer.
