*Frame size* 

- 32x32px: For all the animations.


*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: Die, Dmg, Gallop.

- 200ms: Idle, Gallop.

Notes:
- There isn't a dedicated attack animation because this creature attacks by charging towards its objective.
- Gallop animation can be used at both frame durations, 100ms and 200ms, to get normal walk and charge.
