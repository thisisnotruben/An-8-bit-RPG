*Frame size* 

- 32x32px: For all the animations.


*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: Die, Dmg, Sappling

- 200ms: Heal, Idle, PlatSeed, WakeUp and Walk.



NOTES:

- Shadows: 
The shadows are identical for the 5 different characters included, 
Except for the Sappling animation, which includes separate shadow layers for each character.

- Effects:
The green magical effects are provided as separate layers. You can decide to use them or not depending on the animation style you are looking for.
The Healing animation has 2 separate effect layers. Effect_F is to be placed/rendered over the character, and Effect_B is to be placed/rendered under the character.
The .gif samples display all the effects available.
