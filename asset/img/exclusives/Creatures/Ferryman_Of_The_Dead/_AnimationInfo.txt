*Frame size* 

- 64x64px: For Ferryman and Boat animations.
- 16x16px: For Soul Animations


*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: Attack, Die and Dmg 

- 200ms: Idle, Walk, Row, Soul Appear/Disappear and Soul Floating.


IMPORTANT: When using the boat_only sprites, place/render the layers in this order:

a-Light1 (lantern effect that goes over the character)
b-Boat1 (this part of the boat covers part of the character)
c-Character (Ferryman animations)
d- Light2 (lantern effect that is behind the character)
2-Boat 2 (main body of the boat)
