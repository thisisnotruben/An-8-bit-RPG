*Frame size*

- 32x32px: For all the animations.


*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: Activation_Deactivation, Attack, Die, Dmg, walk.

- 200ms: Idle, Walk.

Notes:
- You can use walk for attacks by charging towards its objective.
- Walk animation can be used at both frame durations, 100ms and 200ms, to get normal walk and charge.
