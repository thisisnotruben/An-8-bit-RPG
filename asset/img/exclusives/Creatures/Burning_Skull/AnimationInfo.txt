This creature has been created with the idea of being a suicidal enemy. 
It does not have a dedicated Attack animation, but 2 different explosion animations. 
One direct explosion (designed to be triggered upon impact with the player)
and one telegraphed explosion (designed to be triggered after missing a direct hit or after X amount of time)

*Frame size* 

- 32x32px: For all the animations.


*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: For all the animations.
