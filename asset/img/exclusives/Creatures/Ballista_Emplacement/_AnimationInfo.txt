*Frame size* 

- 48x48px: For all the animations.


*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: Shot, Dmg, Die.

- 200ms: Reload, Aim.


NOTES:
- This special unit is stationary.
- Ballista must be reloaded before aiming.
- After shooting a bolt, the ballista goes back to the reloading position.
- Because the ballista might be damaged at different moments (during reload or at different aiming directions), there are several Dmg animations available.
- Ballista operator is available in the 6 different humanoid races.
