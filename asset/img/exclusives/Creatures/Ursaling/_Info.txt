There are 12 Ursaling variants:

- 3 Ursaling colours (brown fur/green robe, cream fur/blue robe, and dark fur/khaki robe).
- Each of the above is available with large and small ears.
- Also available as 'Folk' (unarmed and without attack animation) and 'Warrior' (armed with a spear, with the hoodie over the head and including an attack animation).
