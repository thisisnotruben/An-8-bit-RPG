*Frame Size*

- 32x32px: For all the animations.

*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: all Shoot animations, Flamethrower, Dmg, Die
- 200ms: Idle, Scan, Walk.


NOTES for Shoot_Projectile_Impacts:

- Shoot_Projectile_Impacts are a stand-alone effect. You can set the distance that works for your game.
- There are 8 rows. The first 4 rows are the effects for Shoot_Diagonal. The second 4 rows are the effects for Shoot_Orthogonal.
