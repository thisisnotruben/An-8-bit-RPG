*Frame size* 

- 32x32px: For all the animations.


*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: Attack, Die and Dmg.

- 200ms: Idle and Walk.


There are 3 fully animated premade Gnomes ready to use.The addon
also includes separate layers (.png and .aseprite) in different shapes and/or colours to create many more variations. The included parts are:

- Bodies (3 colours)
- Heads (6, 2 saphes X 3 colours)
- Hats (9, 3 shapes X 3 Colours)
