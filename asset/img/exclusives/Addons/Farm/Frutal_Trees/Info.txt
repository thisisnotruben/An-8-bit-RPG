This sprite sheet is sliced in a 32x32px grid.

Each frutal tree type includes 3 different layers to overlay over the 4 tree sprites. From left to right these layer are:
- Flowering
- Fruit hanging from the tree
- Mature fruit on the ground

The fruit types included in the add-on are:
- Apple
- Apricot
- Orange
- Pear
- Lemon
- Pomegranate
- Cherry
