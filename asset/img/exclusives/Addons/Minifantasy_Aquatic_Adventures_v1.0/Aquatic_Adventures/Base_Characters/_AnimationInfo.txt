*Frame size* 

- 32x32px: For all the animations



*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: Attack, Die, Dmg

- 200ms: Idle, Swim, dive, Emerge

* Underwater Idle and Swim animations are common to all characters and are located in the folder called 'Underwater'


NOTES:

- These character animations are provided in 6 water color variants to match the different main water types available in the collection.

- They are available as complete sprites (matching each water type with each race) or as separate layers (bodies and water layers).

- The on land animations for the base characters are available in Minifantasy - Creatures (idle, walk, jump, attack, dmg and die)
