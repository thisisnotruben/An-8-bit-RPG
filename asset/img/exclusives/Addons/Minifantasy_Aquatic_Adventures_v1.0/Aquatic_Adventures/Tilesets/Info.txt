*Frame size*
 
- Slice the sprite sheet in an 8x8px grid
- 8x8px for all tiles
- Check the guidelines to identify the frames for each water type


*Frame Duration*

Adjust to your liking, I recommend 200ms or 300ms



NOTESs:

+ For characters entering in water:

- For the lightest color water, I suggest removing a line of 1 pixel from the bottom for each animation row (to simulate the depth and the character sinking in).

- For the middle color water,  I suggest removing a line of 1 or 2 pixels from the bottom for each animation row (to simulate the depth and the character sinking in).

- The darkest water color, can be treated as an impassable obstacle, or one you need to swim in (in-water animations for base characters and other creatures are included in this asset pack)


+ For the expanded tilesets included in this pack:

- Optional water entrances available for the following tilesets:
	· Forgotten Plains
	· Desolate Desert
	· Icy Wilderness
	· Lost Jungle
	· Gloom Hollows

- All shore edges are considered water entrances for the following tilesets:
	· Desolate Desert (only for the Sandy Shore tiles)
	· Silent Swamp
	· Fae Depths

- Alternatively, you can ignore water entrance tiles completely and consider all shore edges as practicable water entrances

- All the Gloom Hollows water tiles are considered deep water. There are no shallow or medium water tiles included for this tileset.
