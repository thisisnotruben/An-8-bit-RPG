*Frame Duration*

NOTES:

- This creature is provided in 6 variants to match the different main water types available in the collection.

- This creature is amphibious, so it can move both in the water and on land.

- In land animations are in the root folder, while in water animations are in their respective folders.

- Shadow layers are designed to be used in land only
