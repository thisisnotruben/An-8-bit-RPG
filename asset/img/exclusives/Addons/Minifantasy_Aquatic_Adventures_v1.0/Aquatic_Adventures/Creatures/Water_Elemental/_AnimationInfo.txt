*Frame size* 

- 32x32px: For all the animations



*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: Attack, Die, Dmg

- 200ms: Idle and Move



NOTES:

- This creature is provided in 6 water color variants to match the different main water types available in the collection.

- This creature is amphibious, so it can move both in the water and on land.
 
- Move and Die animations have 2 versions, one for when the creature is on land, and another for when the creature is in water. The rest of the animations can be used in both situations.

- Shadow layers are designed to be used in land only.
