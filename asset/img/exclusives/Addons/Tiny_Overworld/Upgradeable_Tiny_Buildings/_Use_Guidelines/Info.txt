This guideline is designed to help you understand how to get the most from these assets.

The groups show the categories of the different elements.

You can check 'Use_Guidelines.png' for quick reference, or overlay 'Use_Guidelines_Layer' on the sprite sheet if you want to have it visible while working with the sprite sheet in your preferred software.

NOTES:
- Each Building has been made with an upgrade progression in mind

- Each Building has 3 stages

- The upgrade direction is top to bottom

- The Resources category makes reference to buildings that traditionally provide or process resources:
	· Farm
	· Mine
	· Sawmill
	· Commercial post
	· Inn / Stables
