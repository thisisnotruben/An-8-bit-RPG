*Frame size* 

- 32x32px: For all the animations.


*Frame Duration*

- 100ms: Throw_Diagonal, Throw_Orthogonal, Dmg

- 200ms: Idle, Walk, Gather


IMPORTANT:

- You can use the Snowball layers if you want your characters to hold a snowball in their hand during an animation
- Snowball layers are tagged with "_f" or "_b" at the end of the name.
- "_f" stands for "front", it should be placed over your character (or be rendered after your character)
- "_b" Stands for "back", it should be placed under your character (or be rendered before your character)
