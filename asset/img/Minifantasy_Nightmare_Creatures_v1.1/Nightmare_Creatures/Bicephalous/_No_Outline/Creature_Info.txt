This creature has been designed to follow a special behaviour:

- It is meant to appear initially with its two-headed version.
- Upon first defeat, the second head becomes a "Slug".
- Slug will try to run away to a safe place.
- Slug will grow into a single-headed version.
- Once the single-headed version is defeated, the creature is gone for good.
* If a slug is killed before growing into a single-headed version,  the creature is also gone for good.
